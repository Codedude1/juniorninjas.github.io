Welcome to Junior Ninjas

Junior Ninjas is a dedicated institution which is expanding access to computer science in schools and increasing participation by young women and students from other underrepresented groups. Our vision is that every student in every school has the opportunity to learn computer science as part of their core education. Our mission is to provide quality education at affordable prices.
